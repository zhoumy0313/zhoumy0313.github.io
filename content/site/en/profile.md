My name is Zhou Mingyang. I received my Bachelor’s degree from the School of Remote Sensing and Information Engineering ([RSGIS](https://rsgis.whu.edu.cn/)), Wuhan University ([WHU](https://www.whu.edu.cn/)). Currently, I am pursuing a Master’s degree in Surveying and Mapping Science and Technology at the Innovation Academy for Precision Measurement Science and Technology ([APM-CAS](https://apm.cas.cn/)), University of Chinese Academy of Sciences ([UCAS](https://www.ucas.ac.cn/)). Meanwhile, I take part in scientific research at the Resilient Navigation Research Group ([R-Nav](https://www.r-navigation.com/)), State Key Laboratory of Information Engineering in Surveying, Mapping and Remote Sensing ([LIESMARS](https://liesmars.whu.edu.cn/)), Wuhan University.

My research mainly focuses on robot localization & navigation and embodied artificial intelligence.
